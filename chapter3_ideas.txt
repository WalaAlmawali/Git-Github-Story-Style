
An ancient shadow sorcerer harvests abandoned memories, growing stronger as identities fade, leaving hollow souls wandering, nameless and lost forever.

Elara discovers a scorched parchment fragment bearing half an ancient rune, hinting at forbidden magic and
 a hidden truth buried in ash.

Elara encounters Squeaky, a witty time-traveling squirrel who speaks in riddles, claiming knowledge of past and future events yet confusing everything.
