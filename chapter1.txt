

The map was never meant to be found. It appeared on a storm-soaked morning, tucked beneath the cracked door of a small cottage at the edge of Brindlewick village.
Elian, a young villager known more for curiosity than caution, discovered it while sweeping away the night’s fallen leaves. The parchment was old, its edges darkened
as if singed by fire, and its ink shimmered faintly like dew in moonlight. Strange symbols curled across its surface, leading toward a place no villager dared speak of—the 
Whispering Woods. Legends said the forest changed paths, swallowed voices, and hid truths the world had forgotten. But the map… was calling.
