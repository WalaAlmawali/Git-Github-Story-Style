The Ancient Runes form a living language, shifting subtly with time and intent. Each symbol holds layers of meaning, readable only through intuition and memory. Spoken aloud, they bend reality, unlocking sealed paths or altering fate. Misuse distorts time itself, trapping careless readers within echoes of their own fractured histories.


The Griffin of the Sky Peaks is a majestic, ancient guardian with keen eyes that perceive truth and deception alike. Its golden wings command the winds, and its roar echoes across mountains. Bound to protect sacred realms, it tests travelers’ courage and purity, allowing only the worthy to pass unharmed.
