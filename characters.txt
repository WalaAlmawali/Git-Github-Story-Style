Elara was a young villager with bright, curious eyes and a restless spirit that always pulled her toward the unknown. Brave beyond her years, she never hesitated to explore forgotten paths or strange rumors.
When she discovered a mysterious, weathered map, her heart raced with excitement rather than fear.

Shadow was a large, silver-black wolf with piercing amber eyes that seemed to read every movement of the 
forest. Fiercely loyal to Elara, he stayed always at her side, silent as mist and swift as wind.
Though fearsome to others, he was gentle with her, a steadfast guardian and friend.
